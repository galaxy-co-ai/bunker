# BUNKER — Asset & World Item Master List

This list is a production inventory of physical assets and diegetic surfaces required to build the facility.
For each item: **Description**, **Purpose in-world**, **Best 2 tools**.

---

## A. Facility Architecture (Environment)

### A1 — Vault Hatch Door (Primary Entrance)
- **Description:** Heavy circular/segmented hatch with locking lugs, gasket, warning placards, inspection plate.
- **Purpose:** Establishes containment doctrine and physical authority at first contact.
- **Tools:** Blender • Substance 3D Painter

### A2 — Airlock Corridor Kit (Modular)
- **Description:** Wall/floor/ceiling modules, bulkhead frames, seals, conduits, vent panels, service hatches.
- **Purpose:** Forms the facility spine; supports expansion without redesign.
- **Tools:** Blender • Unreal Engine 5

### A3 — Control Room Shell (Command Theater)
- **Description:** Primary room geometry: raised console floor, cable trenches, ceiling baffles, observation window.
- **Purpose:** The “home screen” is a room; operator’s command presence.
- **Tools:** Unreal Engine 5 • Blender

### A4 — Equipment Bay Shells (Rack Alcoves)
- **Description:** Recessed bays, rack rails, access plates, cable routing, service lighting.
- **Purpose:** Makes compute and storage physically legible.
- **Tools:** Blender • Unreal Engine 5

### A5 — Maintenance Shop Shell
- **Description:** Workbench area, tool wall, parts shelves, floor drains, grit mats, signage.
- **Purpose:** Supports upgrades/repairs as canon actions.
- **Tools:** Blender • Unreal Engine 5
