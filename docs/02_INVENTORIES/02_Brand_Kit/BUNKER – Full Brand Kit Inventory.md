# BUNKER — Full Brand Kit Inventory (Inventory Only)

This is a complete inventory of the brand/world kit required to ship BUNKER as a coherent AAA-grade facility universe.
No visuals are produced here. This is scope and governance.

---

## 1. Core Marks (Insignia System)
- **Primary Hatch Sigil** (facility insignia; not a "logo")
- **Hatch Sigil — State Variants**
  - Stable
  - Processing
  - Degraded
  - Fault
  - Offline
- **Department Seals / Stamps** (ink-stamp style)
  - OPS (Operations)
  - POWER (Distribution)
  - COMMS (Uplink/Relay)
  - ARCHIVE (Storage)
  - MAINT (Maintenance)
  - SECURITY (Access)
  - LAB (Research)
- **Restricted Wordmark: "BUNKER"**
  - Allowed contexts: boot/init, facility overview, documentation title blocks
  - Disallowed contexts: persistent UI chrome, decorative branding

---

## 2. Color System (Functional Palette)
- **Signature Accent: Vault Yellow/Gold** (NON-NEGOTIABLE)
  - THE color that makes BUNKER read as BUNKER
  - Safety railings, door trim, zone numerals, floor markings
  - Yellow against steel grey is the foundational contrast pairing
- **Structural Neutrals**
  - Steel blue-grey range (plate steel → brushed metal → dark iron)
  - Concrete range (clean pour → stained → patched)
  - Rubber/Polymer blacks (functional, not design)
- **Lighting-Derived Color (The Warmth)**
  - Tungsten warm: desk lamps, work lights, residential pools
  - Fluorescent cool: overhead panels, corridors
  - Mixed lighting doctrine: BUNKER is not cold and sterile
- **CRT Phosphor Accents**
  - Phosphor green / amber (primary emission)
  - Cyan/blue (secondary data)
  - Rules for scanline/noise/bloom intensity
- **State Colors (Doctrine)**
  - GREEN SOLID: Stable / Ready
  - AMBER PULSE: Processing / Active
  - DIM: Idle / Standby
  - RED FLICKER: Fault / Critical / Denied
- **Warning Bands**
  - Caution (Yellow/Black diagonal)
  - Warning (Orange/Black)
  - Danger (Red/White)
  - Restricted (Red/Black)
- **Material-Derived Color**
  - Rust/oxidation at wear points (controlled, realistic)
  - Brass/bronze on legacy equipment
- **Contrast & Readability Rules**
  - Minimum contrast requirements for terminals
  - "Color is never the only signal" redundancy doctrine

---

## 3. Typography System
- **Placard/Heading Typeface**
  - Condensed industrial sans for room labels, module headers, signage
- **Terminal/Telemetry Typeface**
  - Monospace for logs, routing tables, metrics, identifiers
- **Numeric Doctrine**
  - Tabular numbers
  - Fixed-width alignment rules for telemetry
- **Copy Tone Rules**
  - Imperative/procedural
  - Declarative system reporting
  - No conversational filler

## 5. Texture & Material Library
- **Concrete Surfaces**
  - Clean, stained, cracked, patched
- **Painted Steel**
  - Fresh paint, scuffed edges, chipped corners, primer exposure
- **Rust/Oxidation Layers**
  - Light oxidation → heavy flake (controlled; not cartoon)
- **Rubber & Gasket Materials**
  - Door seals, cable grommets, anti-fatigue mats
- **CRT/Glass Overlays**
  - Dust, fingerprints, subtle scratches
  - Scanline & phosphor persistence overlays
- **Paper Stock & Ink**
  - Manual pages, incident forms, stamps, coffee stains, folds

---

## 6. Architectural Grammar (Facility Geometry)
- **Rounded Hatch Doorways**
  - Oval/rounded steel frames with thick rims
  - Pressure-vessel geometry, not rectangular modern doors
  - Bulkhead aesthetic with visible locking mechanisms
- **Vault Door (The Ceremony)**
  - Massive gear-door with bold facility numeral
  - Hydraulic arms, yellow safety railings
  - THE entry moment; authority entering containment
- **Multi-Level Depth**
  - Catwalks, railings, atriums
  - Views that show facility scale and engineering
- **Exposed Infrastructure**
  - Conduits, pipes, cable trays visible on ceilings
  - Service panels, junction boxes, vent grilles

---

## 7. Humanizing Elements (The Lived Space)

**Critical: BUNKER is not a sterile hospital. People LIVE here.**

- **Plant Life**
  - Potted plants, ferns in common areas
  - Not decorative — survival/morale function
- **Furniture**
  - Chairs, benches, tables in circulation areas
  - Worn but maintained
- **Personal Artifacts**
  - Items on desks, notice boards
  - Evidence of daily operation
- **Operational Signage**
  - Wet floor signs, "LAUNDRY" labels
  - Functional markers of lived reality

---

## 8. Layout & Composition Doctrine
- **Grid System (Engineered Panels)**
  - Panel bays, label gutters, fixed telemetry columns
- **Bezel/Plate Grammar**
  - Outer plates, inner insets, fastener patterns
- **Divider Grammar**
  - Hairline rules, riveted bars, warning tape dividers
- **Information Hierarchy**
  - State > Action > Detail > Archive
- **Labeling Rules**
  - IDs, revisions, timestamps
  - Location path formatting (Wing → Room → Console)

---

## 9. Motion Language
- **Timing Doctrine**
  - Deliberate starts, inertial settling, minimal easing theatrics
- **Transition Types**
  - Wipe, scan, slide, stamp, relay-flicker
- **Heavy Motion (Vault Door)**
  - Hydraulic movement, mechanical weight
  - The door OPENS — this is ceremony
- **State Animations**
  - Stable: steady lamp
  - Processing: controlled pulse
  - Degraded: slow warning cadence
  - Fault: strong cadence with hard cutoff
- **Prohibited Motion**
  - Bouncy UI
  - Elastic overshoot
  - Decorative confetti/celebrations

---

## 10. Audio Identity
- **Ambient Bed**
  - Ventilation hum, distant mechanical variance
- **Interaction Foley**

- **Alert Ladder**

- **Silence Rules**
  - When audio must not play (stealth/precision contexts)
